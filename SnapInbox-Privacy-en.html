Privacy Policy

SnapInbox On-device only

Last updated: 2025-12-15

Overview
SnapInbox (“the App”) helps you manage screenshots locally on your device. We take your privacy seriously.

Data Collection

SnapInbox does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

  • No account system
  • No analytics SDKs
  • No third-party tracking
  • No cloud upload

Photos Access

The App requests access to your Photos library only to identify screenshots and show them in a temporary inbox.

  • All processing happens on-device
  • Your photos are never uploaded to any server
  • Deleting photos requires explicit user confirmation

If you choose Limited Photos Access, the App can only access the items you explicitly allow.

Data Storage

App data (such as processed status and settings) is stored locally on your device using system-provided storage mechanisms. SnapInbox does not sync or back up your data to any external server.

Children’s Privacy

SnapInbox does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
