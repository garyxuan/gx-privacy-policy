1. 我们收集的信息

我们仅在实现应用功能所必需的范围内处理信息：

  • 你主动输入的内容：菜谱名称、食材、步骤、烹饪时间、口味标签、备注。
  • 你主动选择的图片：菜谱封面图与步骤图片（来自系统相册选择）。
  • 设备和服务相关信息：用于保障稳定性、同步和广告展示（可能由系统或第三方 SDK 处理）。

2. 信息如何使用

  • 提供核心功能：创建、编辑、展示、搜索和随机推荐菜谱。
  • 在你可用且启用 iCloud 的情况下，通过 CloudKit 私有数据库进行同步。
  • 展示应用内广告与改进产品稳定性、性能。

3. 权限说明

相册权限

应用会在你选择上传菜谱图片时请求相册权限。若你拒绝授权，仍可使用大部分非图片功能。

4. 数据存储与同步

  • 菜谱数据默认保存在设备本地。
  • 当系统 iCloud 可用时，数据可能同步至你 Apple ID 对应的 iCloud 私有容器（CloudKit Private Database）。
  • 我们不提供公开社区发布功能，不会把你的菜谱公开给其他用户。

5. 第三方服务

我们使用以下第三方服务以支持应用能力：

  • Apple CloudKit（数据私有同步）
  • Google Mobile Ads（广告展示）

相关隐私政策可参考：

  • Google Privacy Policy

7. 数据共享与披露

除以下情形外，我们不会出售你的个人信息：

  • 为实现功能而必要的第三方服务处理（如 CloudKit、广告 SDK）；
  • 遵守法律法规、司法程序或监管要求；
  • 为保护用户、开发者或公众的安全与合法权益。

8. 数据保留与删除

  • 你可在应用内删除菜谱与相关内容。
  • 如使用 iCloud 同步，iCloud 中的数据管理受 Apple 账户体系和规则约束。
  • 卸载应用不会自动删除你 iCloud 私有数据库中的所有历史数据。

1. Information We Collect

We process information only as needed to provide app features:

  • Content you provide: recipe names, ingredients, steps, cooking time, taste tags, and notes.
  • Images you choose: cover and step photos selected from your photo library.
  • Device/service data: used for stability, sync, and ad delivery (possibly processed by system APIs or third-party SDKs).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core features: create, edit, display, search, and randomize recipes.
  • To sync data with your private CloudKit database when iCloud is available.
  • To display in-app ads and improve reliability and performance.

3. Permissions

Photo Library

We request Photo Library access only when you choose recipe images. If denied, most non-photo features remain available.

4. Storage and Sync

  • Recipe data is primarily stored on your device.
  • When iCloud is available, data may sync to your Apple ID private iCloud container (CloudKit Private Database).
  • We do not provide a public publishing feed for your recipes.

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to support app capabilities:

  • Apple CloudKit (private data sync)
  • Google Mobile Ads (ad delivery)

References:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

6. Advertising and Identifiers

This app contains native ad placements. Ad providers may process identifiers, usage data, and diagnostics for ad delivery and measurement. Actual scope depends on third-party SDK policies and settings.

7. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose data only when necessary:

  • To service providers needed for functionality (for example CloudKit and ad SDKs);
  • To comply with applicable laws, legal process, or regulatory requests;
  • To protect rights, safety, and legitimate interests of users, developer, or the public.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can delete recipes and related content inside the app.
  • If iCloud sync is used, iCloud data handling is also subject to Apple account systems and rules.
  • Uninstalling the app may not automatically remove all records from private iCloud storage.

9. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information without consent, please contact us.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, but no storage or network system can be guaranteed as absolutely secure.
